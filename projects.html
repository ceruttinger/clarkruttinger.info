Projects

This page preserves earlier public R/Shiny and R Markdown projects. These examples are older than my most recent client-facing analytics work, but they show long-running experience building public data products, reproducible analyses, and interactive tools in the R ecosystem.

More recent proprietary client dashboards, code, and internal GlobalData deliverables are not posted here.

Public R/Shiny and R Markdown examples

Utah Mental Health Workforce, 2021

Project type: Interactive workforce report and projection model components.
Role: Designed and administered a statewide survey of LCSWs, CMHCs, MFTs, and psychologists licensed in Utah. Developed initial chart and layout examples for the team and coded the projection model section of the report.
What it demonstrates: survey design, workforce analytics, projection modeling, public-sector reporting, and R/Shiny-based communication of technical findings.
Link: Utah Mental Health Workforce 2021

National Nursing by the Numbers Dashboard

Project type: Public dashboard companion to national nursing workforce survey analysis.
Role: Coauthored analysis of the NCSBN 2020 National Nurse Sample Survey and designed/coded a public flexdashboard using respondent state-of-employment summaries after the report was released.
What it demonstrates: R/flexdashboard development, survey data summarization, public-facing dashboard design, reproducible reporting, and translation of national survey findings into an interactive data product.
Links: Dashboard | Related journal article

Physician Ratios Dashboard

Project type: Interactive benchmark dashboard.
Role: Built a dashboard comparing Utah physician specialty ratios to AAMC national physician specialty ratio benchmarks from 2010 to 2020.
What it demonstrates: workforce benchmarking, interactive reporting, R-based dashboard development, and clear communication of specialty distribution patterns.
Link: Physician Ratios Dashboard

RN Data Analysis in R

Project type: Reproducible R Markdown analysis for public communication.
Role: Analyzed Utah RN wage data from workforce surveys conducted between 2015 and 2020. The analysis supported an investigative report on nursing and COVID-19 in the Deseret News.
What it demonstrates: R Markdown, survey analytics, workforce wage analysis, reproducible documentation, and public-interest data communication.
Links: R Markdown analysis | Related Deseret News article
